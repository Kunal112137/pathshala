About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala Foundation is a community-driven non-profit organization working tirelessly to bridge the educational gap for children in marginalized communities.

Founded in 2020, our journey began with a simple mission — to ensure that every child, regardless of their economic background, has access to quality education. What started as a handful of passionate volunteers teaching a few children in a small slum area has now grown into a structured educational movement with outreach across multiple settlements.

We offer free learning sessions, basic literacy programs, and support for school admissions. We also run art, drama, and computer classes to foster creativity and digital literacy among children who otherwise would never get such exposure.

Our team comprises dedicated volunteers, educators, and youth leaders who conduct regular workshops, events, and mentoring sessions. Beyond education, we also focus on emotional well-being and life skills development to ensure holistic growth.

With the support of generous donors and selfless volunteers, we have impacted the lives of over 30,000 children and their families. We believe that real change begins with education — and every effort counts.

Join us in lighting up minds and building a better future, one child at a time.
